FINLANDIA VODKA

1- A LESS ORDINARY QUIZ

This quiz was designed as a social extension of Finlandia’s global campaign that toasts to “a life less ordinary.” Fans that “like” the Finlandia Vodka Facebook page can answer a series of questions to reveal which “less ordinary” character they relate to in the world of Finlandia.




2- THE CAPTURE A LESS ORDINARY MOMENT APP

This application was designed as a social/camera extension of Finlandia’s global campaign that toasts to “a life less ordinary.” Users could capture an amazing moment in time and then Finlandia's app would generate a toast that would pair to that moment. The final photo could be shared or saved onto a users phone.
